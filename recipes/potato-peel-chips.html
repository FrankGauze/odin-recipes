Ingredients

  • Potato peels
  • Salt
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Rinse the potato peels thoroughly to remove any dirt or debris.
  2. Toss the potato peels with salt and your choice of spices in a bowl, ensuring they are evenly coated.
  3. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat and add the seasoned potato peels in a single layer.
  4. Fry the potato peels until they are golden and crispy, making sure to flip them occasionally for even cooking.
  5. Remove the crispy potato peel chips from the pan and drain them on a paper towel to remove excess oil.
  6. Sprinkle with an extra pinch of salt and spices if desired, and savor the crunchy goodness of the Crispy Potato Peel Delight, a testament to the beauty of turning kitchen scraps into a delectable snack.
