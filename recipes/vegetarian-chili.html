Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Tomatoes
  • Bell peppers
  • Onions
  • Spices (e.g., chili powder, cumin, paprika)

Steps

  1. In a large pot, combine beans, tomatoes, bell peppers, onions, and spices.
  2. Bring the pot to a gentle simmer over medium heat, stirring occasionally to ensure the flavors meld together.
  3. Let the chili cook until the vegetables are tender and the flavors have developed.
  4. Adjust seasoning to taste, and let the chili simmer for a few more minutes to enhance the flavors.
  5. Serve the Harmony in a Bowl Vegetarian Chili hot, topped with a dollop of sour cream or a sprinkle of cheese, and savor the comforting and flavorful goodness of this delightful one-pot meal.
