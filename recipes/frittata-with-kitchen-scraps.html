Ingredients

  • Leftover vegetables
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to a moderate temperature.
  2. In a skillet, sauté the leftover vegetables until they are tender and fragrant.
  3. In a bowl, whisk the eggs and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Pour the eggs over the vegetables in the skillet, allowing them to set slightly.
  5. Sprinkle cheese over the frittata mixture and transfer the skillet to the oven.
  6. Bake until the frittata is golden and set, then remove from the oven and let it cool slightly before slicing and serving the Rustic Kitchen Scraps Frittata Delight, a testament to the beauty of transforming kitchen scraps into a culinary masterpiece.
