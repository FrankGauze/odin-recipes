Ingredients

  • Canned chickpeas
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Coconut milk
  • Blend of spices

Steps

  1. In a large pot, sauté onions and garlic until fragrant and translucent.
  2. Add the canned tomatoes and coconut milk to the pot, stirring to combine and bring to a gentle simmer.
  3. Incorporate the canned chickpeas into the simmering mixture, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  4. Sprinkle in the blend of spices, adjusting to taste preferences and ensuring a harmonious balance of flavors.
  5. Let the Fragrant Chickpea Curry Delight simmer gently, allowing the aromas to develop and the curry to thicken to perfection.
  6. Serve the curry over a bed of fluffy rice for a complete and filling meal that celebrates the beauty of bold flavors and comforting textures.
