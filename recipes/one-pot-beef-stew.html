Ingredients

  • Beef chunks
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Peas
  • Savory broth

Steps

  1. In a large pot, combine beef chunks, potatoes, carrots, peas, and savory broth.
  2. Bring the pot to a gentle simmer over medium heat, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  3. Let the stew cook until the beef is tender and the vegetables are cooked to perfection.
  4. Adjust seasoning to taste, and let the stew simmer for a few more minutes to enhance the flavors.
  5. Serve the Cozy Hearth Beef Stew piping hot, accompanied by a slice of crusty bread or a sprinkle of fresh herbs, and savor the comforting and heartwarming essence of this delightful one-pot creation.
