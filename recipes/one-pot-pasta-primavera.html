Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Seasonal vegetables (e.g., bell peppers, zucchini, cherry tomatoes)
  • Herbs (e.g., basil, oregano)
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. In a large pot, combine water, pasta, seasonal vegetables, herbs, and a drizzle of olive oil.
  2. Bring the pot to a gentle simmer over medium heat, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.
  3. Let the pasta and vegetables cook together until the pasta is al dente and the vegetables are tender.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and garnish with fresh herbs before serving.
  5. Enjoy the Harmony in a Pot Pasta, a delightful medley of flavors and colors that celebrates the beauty of seasonal produce and the joy of simple, one-pot cooking.
