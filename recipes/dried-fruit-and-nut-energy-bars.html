Ingredients

  • Dried fruits
  • Nuts
  • Oats
  • Honey

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the dried fruits, nuts, and oats, ensuring an even distribution of ingredients.
  2. Drizzle the honey over the mixture, using a spatula to fold and incorporate the honey until everything is well coated.
  3. Press the mixture into a lined pan, using a spatula to flatten and compact the ingredients.
  4. Refrigerate the pan for a few hours to allow the energy bars to set and firm up.
  5. Once set, remove the pan from the refrigerator and cut the mixture into individual bars of your desired size.
  6. Indulge in the Wholesome Dried Fruit and Nut Energy Bars as a quick and nutritious snack on the go, savoring the blend of flavors and textures that offer a burst of energy and satisfaction in every bite.
