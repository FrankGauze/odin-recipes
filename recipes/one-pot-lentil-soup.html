Ingredients

  • Lentils
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Onions
  • Broth

Steps

  1. In a large pot, combine lentils, carrots, celery, onions, and broth.
  2. Bring the pot to a gentle simmer over medium heat, allowing the ingredients to meld together.
  3. Let the soup cook until the lentils are tender and the flavors have developed.
  4. Adjust seasoning to taste, and let the soup simmer for a few more minutes to enhance the flavors.
  5. Serve the Harmony in a Bowl Lentil Soup hot, garnished with a sprinkle of fresh herbs or a drizzle of olive oil, and relish in the nourishing and comforting goodness of this delightful one-pot creation.
