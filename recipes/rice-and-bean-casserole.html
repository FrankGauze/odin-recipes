Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Canned beans (any variety)
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Spices (such as cumin, paprika, garlic powder, and oregano)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and grease a casserole dish.
  2. In a bowl, combine the rice, drained canned beans, canned tomatoes, and a mix of spices, ensuring they are evenly distributed.
  3. Transfer the mixture into the prepared casserole dish, spreading it out into an even layer.
  4. Cover the dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for about 45-50 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together and the rice to cook through.
  5. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until the top is slightly golden and the casserole is bubbling.
  6. Let the casserole cool slightly before serving, allowing the flavors to settle and the dish to reach its peak of deliciousness. Enjoy the comforting embrace of this Hearty Rice and Bean Casserole with loved ones, creating memories that linger long after the last bite.
