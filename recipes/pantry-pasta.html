Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Canned tomato sauce
  • Canned tuna
  • Dried herbs (such as basil, oregano, and thyme)

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions until it reaches al dente perfection, then drain and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan, heat the canned tomato sauce over medium heat, adding in the canned tuna and a sprinkle of dried herbs to infuse the sauce with flavor.
  3. Allow the sauce to simmer gently for a few minutes, letting the aromas of the herbs and tuna meld together harmoniously.
  4. Once the sauce is heated through and seasoned to your liking, add the cooked pasta to the saucepan, tossing everything together to ensure the pasta is coated evenly.
  5. Season the Pantry Pasta with additional herbs and spices to taste, adjusting the flavors to suit your preferences.
  6. Serve the Comforting Pantry Pasta hot, savoring each forkful of this simple yet satisfying dish that celebrates the beauty of pantry staples and the joy of a well-prepared meal.
