Wanderer's Wild Foraged Greens Omelette

Description

Indulge in the flavors of the wilderness with our Wanderer's Wild Foraged Greens Omelette, a wholesome dish made with eggs and foraged greens like dandelion leaves or wild spinach. Cooked in a cast-iron skillet over the campfire, this omelette is a nutritious and filling option for breakfast or any meal. Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Foraged greens (such as dandelion leaves or wild spinach)
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk until well combined.
  2. Wash and chop the foraged greens into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Heat a cast-iron skillet over the campfire.
  4. Pour the whisked eggs into the skillet and let them set slightly.
  5. Sprinkle the chopped greens over one half of the omelette.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Carefully fold the other half of the omelette over the greens.
  8. Cook until the eggs are fully set and the omelette is golden brown.
  9. Serve the Wanderer's Wild Foraged Greens Omelette hot, savoring the flavors of the wilderness in each nourishing bite.
