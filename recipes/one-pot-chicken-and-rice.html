Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Rice
  • Broth
  • Spices (e.g., cumin, paprika, garlic powder)

Steps

  1. In a large pot, combine chicken pieces, rice, broth, and spices.
  2. Bring the pot to a gentle simmer over medium heat, stirring occasionally to ensure even cooking.
  3. Let the chicken and rice cook together until the rice is tender and the chicken is cooked through.
  4. Adjust seasoning to taste, and let the flavors meld together for a few more minutes.
  5. Serve the Cozy Harmony Chicken and Rice hot, garnished with fresh herbs or a squeeze of lemon, and savor the comforting flavors of this delightful one-pot meal that brings warmth and harmony to your table.
