Ingredients

  • Quinoa
  • Canned corn
  • Canned beans
  • Vinaigrette dressing

Steps

  1. Cook the quinoa according to package instructions, ensuring it is fluffy and ready to be the star of your salad.
  2. Drain and rinse the canned corn and beans to remove excess salt and preservatives, then add them to a large mixing bowl.
  3. Once the quinoa is cooked, add it to the bowl with the corn and beans, gently tossing to combine all the ingredients.
  4. Drizzle the vinaigrette dressing over the salad, ensuring each component is coated in the tangy goodness.
  5. Toss the salad gently to distribute the dressing evenly and allow the flavors to meld together.
  6. Chill the Wholesome Quinoa Salad with Canned Vegetables in the refrigerator for a while to let the flavors marry, or enjoy it at room temperature for a cozy meal that celebrates the beauty of simplicity and nourishment.
