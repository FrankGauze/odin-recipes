Ingredients

  • Foraged wild mushrooms (morels, chanterelles, porcini)
  • Arborio rice
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Vegetable broth
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Butter
  • White wine
  • Fresh herbs (optional for garnish)

  5. Gradually add vegetable broth, stirring constantly, until the rice is creamy and al dente.
  6. Fold in the foraged wild mushrooms and cook until they release their earthy flavors into the risotto.
  7. Finish by stirring in butter, Parmesan cheese, and any additional seasonings to taste.
  8. Serve the Forest Bounty Wild Mushroom Risotto hot, garnished with fresh herbs if desired, and savor the essence of the forest in every delectable bite.
