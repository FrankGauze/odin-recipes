Ingredients

  • Foraged stinging nettles
  • Potatoes
  • Onion
  • Vegetable or chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Carefully handle and wash the stinging nettles, removing any tough stems.
  2. In a large pot, saut√© chopped onions in olive oil until translucent.
  3. Add diced potatoes and cook until slightly softened.
  4. Pour in the vegetable or chicken broth and bring to a gentle simmer.
  5. Add the stinging nettles to the pot and let them wilt into the soup.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and let the flavors meld together over low heat.
  7. Serve the Verdant Harvest Nettle Soup hot, accompanied by a slice of crusty bread or a drizzle of olive oil, and savor the nourishing and earthy essence of this delightful creation from the wild.
