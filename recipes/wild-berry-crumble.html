Enchanted Forest Wild Berry Crumble

Ingredients

  • Foraged blackberries
  • Foraged raspberries
  • Foraged huckleberries
  • Oats
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Ice cream (for serving)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a baking dish.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the blackberries, raspberries, and huckleberries.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine oats, flour, sugar, cinnamon, and melted butter to create the crumble topping.
  4. Spread the mixed berries evenly in the greased baking dish.
  5. Sprinkle the crumble topping over the berries, covering them completely.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the berries are bubbling.
  7. Serve the Enchanted Forest Wild Berry Crumble warm, topped with a scoop of ice cream for a truly enchanting dessert experience.
